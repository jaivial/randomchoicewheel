Help & How-To Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Decision Wheel Spinner! This comprehensive guide will help you make the most of our decision-making tool. Whether you're new to wheel spinners or looking to discover advanced features, this guide has you covered.

How to Use the Decision Wheel

Step 1: Add Your Options

  1. Locate the "Add Options to Spin Wheel" section on the left side of the page
  2. Type your first option in the text input field
  3. Click the "Add" button or press Enter on your keyboard
  4. Repeat this process for each option you want to include
  5. You can add as many options as you need - the wheel will automatically adjust
üí° Pro Tip: Be specific with your options. Instead of "food," try "pizza at Mario's" for clearer results!

Step 2: Review Your Options

  1. Check the options list below the input field
  2. Each option appears as a colorful item in your list
  3. Use the "X" button next to any option to remove it if needed
  4. Make sure all options are relevant to your decision

Step 3: Spin the Wheel

  1. Once you have at least one option, the "Spin the Wheel!" button becomes active
  2. Click the purple "Spin the Wheel!" button to start the selection process
  3. Watch as the wheel spins and gradually slows down
  4. The wheel will stop and highlight your randomly selected result

Step 4: View Your Result

  1. A popup window will display your winning result with celebration effects
  2. Read the selected option carefully
  3. Use the "Spin Again" button if you want another random selection
  4. Click "Close" to return to the main wheel

Advanced Tips and Tricks

Increasing Probability for Certain Options

If you want certain options to have a higher chance of being selected:

  • Add the same option multiple times to the wheel
  • For example, add "Pizza" three times and "Salad" once
  • This gives pizza a 75% chance of being selected

Managing Large Lists

For wheels with many options:

  • The wheel automatically adjusts segment sizes for readability
  • Text may become smaller with more options - this is normal
  • Consider grouping similar options to keep the wheel manageable
  • Use the "Clear All" button to start fresh when needed

Mobile Device Usage

Using the spinner on your phone or tablet:

  • The wheel automatically resizes to fit your screen
  • Tap the input field to bring up your device's keyboard
  • All buttons are touch-friendly and properly spaced
  • The experience is identical to desktop usage

Troubleshooting

The Spin Button Won't Work

  • Make sure you've added at least one option to the wheel
  • Check that your browser has JavaScript enabled
  • Try refreshing the page if the button remains inactive
  • Ensure your browser is up to date

My Options Disappeared

  • Options are stored locally in your browser
  • Clearing browser data will remove saved options
  • Private/incognito mode doesn't save options between sessions
  • Different browsers don't share the same saved options

The Wheel Looks Different on My Device

  • This is normal - the design adapts to different screen sizes
  • Functionality remains the same across all devices
  • Colors and animations work best on modern browsers
  • Consider updating your browser for the best experience

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the wheel spinner truly random?

A: Yes! We use sophisticated algorithms to ensure completely random and unbiased results. Each option has an equal probability of being selected (unless you've added duplicates intentionally).

Q: Do you store my wheel options?

A: No, we don't store your options on our servers. Everything is saved locally in your browser for privacy. Your data never leaves your device.

Q: How many options can I add?

A: There's no hard limit, but for best visual results, we recommend keeping it under 20 options. The wheel automatically adjusts, but readability may decrease with too many options.

Q: Can I save my wheels for later use?

A: Currently, options are saved automatically in your browser. You can return to find your last wheel setup, but only on the same device and browser.

Q: Does this work on mobile devices?

A: Absolutely! The wheel spinner is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Q: Is this free to use?

A: Yes, Decision Wheel Spinner is completely free to use. No registration, downloads, or payments required.

Q: Can I use this for commercial purposes?

A: You can use our tool for personal, educational, and most commercial purposes. Please review our Terms of Service for specific guidelines.

Best Practices for Decision Making

Before You Spin

  • Make sure all options are genuinely acceptable to you
  • Consider if any options need more information before deciding
  • Remove options you're not actually willing to choose
  • Think about whether timing matters for any options

After You Spin

  • Pay attention to your gut reaction to the result
  • If you feel disappointed, that option might not be right
  • If you feel relieved or excited, that's a good sign
  • Remember that you can always spin again if needed
